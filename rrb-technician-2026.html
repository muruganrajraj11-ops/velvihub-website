🚆 RRB Technician Recruitment 2026

📢 Organization : Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)

📌 Advertisement No : CEN 02/2026

💼 Posts : Technician Grade I Signal & Technician Grade III

👥 Total Vacancies : 6,565 Posts

📍 Job Location : All India

💰 Salary : ₹19,900 – ₹29,200 + Allowances

📅 Apply Online : 30-06-2026

⏰ Last Date : 29-07-2026

📋 Vacancy Details

  • Technician Grade I Signal
  • Technician Grade III
  • Total Vacancies : 6,565 Posts

🎓 Educational Qualification

  • Technician Grade I Signal – B.Sc / Diploma / Engineering (Relevant Discipline)
  • Technician Grade III – ITI / NCVT / SCVT (Relevant Trade)
  • Candidates should refer to the official notification for post-wise eligibility.

🎂 Age Limit

  • Minimum Age : 18 Years
  • Technician Grade III : Up to 33 Years
  • Technician Grade I Signal : Up to 36 Years
  • Age Relaxation Applicable as per Government Rules.

💰 Salary Details

  • Technician Grade III – Pay Level 2 (₹19,900 + Allowances)
  • Technician Grade I Signal – Pay Level 5 (₹29,200 + Allowances)

📝 Selection Process

  • ✅ Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • ✅ Document Verification (DV)
  • ✅ Medical Examination
  • ✅ Final Merit List

💳 Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS : ₹500
  • SC / ST / PwBD / Women / Ex-Servicemen / Minorities / EBC : ₹250
  • Fee must be paid through Online Mode.
  • Eligible candidates receive fee refunds after appearing for the CBT as per RRB rules.

📅 Important Dates

  • 📢 Notification Released : 18-05-2026
  • 🟢 Online Application Starts : 30-06-2026
  • 🔴 Last Date to Apply : 29-07-2026
  • 💳 Last Date for Fee Payment : As per RRB Schedule
  • 📝 CBT Exam Date : Will be Announced
  • 🎫 Admit Card : Before Examination

📂 Required Documents

  • 10th Marksheet
  • ITI / Diploma / Degree Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Community Certificate (If Applicable)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
  • Signature
  • Valid Mobile Number & Email ID

🖥️ How To Apply

  1. Visit the Official RRB Apply Portal.
  2. Complete One Time Registration (OTR).
  3. Login using your Registration Details.
  4. Fill the Online Application Form Carefully.
  5. Upload Photo & Signature.
  6. Pay the Application Fee.
  7. Submit the Application.
  8. Download & Print the Application Form.
