The History of Tea in Valparai

Valparai is one of India's most beautiful tea-growing regions. During the nineteenth century, British planters identified the cool climate, fertile soil and abundant rainfall of the Anamalai Hills as ideal for commercial tea cultivation.

Over time, vast tea estates were established across the hills. Today, these estates not only produce high-quality tea but also contribute to the unique identity and economy of Valparai.

Beginning of Tea Cultivation

Tea cultivation gradually replaced coffee plantations in many areas. Estate development brought factories, roads, bridges and workers' settlements, shaping modern Valparai.

Tea Manufacturing Process

The journey of tea begins with carefully plucking fresh leaves from the tea bushes. The leaves then pass through several carefully controlled stages before becoming the tea we enjoy.

Step 1 - Plucking

Skilled workers hand-pick the tender tea leaves to ensure the highest quality.

Step 2 - Withering

Fresh leaves are spread out to reduce moisture and prepare them for rolling.

Step 3 - Rolling, Drying & Packing

The leaves are rolled, oxidized, dried, graded and finally packed before reaching consumers around the world.

Famous Tea Estates of Valparai

Valparai is home to several renowned tea estates that produce premium-quality tea while preserving the natural beauty of the Anamalai Hills.

🏞 Tata Tea Estates

One of the largest and most well-known tea plantation groups in Valparai, recognised for sustainable tea cultivation and environmental conservation.

🍃 Scenic Tea Gardens

The rolling green tea gardens of Valparai attract thousands of visitors every year and provide breathtaking views across the surrounding hills.

The People Behind Every Cup of Tea

Thousands of dedicated workers contribute to the tea industry in Valparai. Their knowledge, hard work and commitment make it possible to produce high-quality tea every day.

🍃 Skilled Tea Pluckers

Tea leaves are carefully hand-picked to ensure quality. The experience and skill of tea workers play an important role in producing premium tea.

🏭 Tea Factory Teams

After harvesting, factory workers oversee withering, rolling, drying, grading and packing, ensuring every batch meets high quality standards.
