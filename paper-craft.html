✂️ What is Paper Craft?

✂️ Paper Craft is the art of making beautiful objects, toys, decorations, and creative projects using paper. Children can cut, fold, paste, and decorate paper to create amazing crafts.

🎨 Paper Craft improves creativity, fine motor skills, problem-solving, hand-eye coordination, and imagination through fun learning activities.
