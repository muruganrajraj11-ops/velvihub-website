Development + Operations = Faster Software Delivery

📖 What is DevOps?

DevOps என்பது Development Team மற்றும் Operations Team இணைந்து வேகமாக Software உருவாக்கி Deploy செய்யும் முறையாகும்.

DevOps = Development + Operations

🎯 Why DevOps?

✔ Faster Deployment

✔ Better Collaboration

✔ Automation

✔ Reduced Errors

✔ Continuous Delivery
